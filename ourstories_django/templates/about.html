About HaitianStories

HaitianStories is an open-source collaboration of UNICEF and partners designed to collect stories from young people via computer with internet connection or mobile phone. Once stories are collected they are shown on a map and can be sorted by age, language, country of origin, theme, etc.

Further development will extend the languages, moderation abilities and functionalities of Our Stories.

Please join us in recording your story today.
