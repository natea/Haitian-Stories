About Haitian Stories

{% blocktrans %}

HaitianStories is a project that was started at the CrisisCamp Boston in response to the crisis in Haiti. HaitianStories is built on top of OurStories, an open-source collaboration of UNICEF and partners designed to collect stories from young people via computer with internet connection or mobile phone. Once stories are collected they are shown on a map and can be sorted by age, language, country of origin, theme, etc.

You can contact the project lead Nate Aune at natea (at) jazkarta (dot) com. {% endblocktrans %}
